Returns the value of a parameter on the parent synth by attribute index. The standard indices are 0 (Gain), 1 (Balance), 2 (VoiceLimit), and 3 (KillFadeTime). Subclasses such as samplers extend this with additional parameters at higher indices. To read attributes of other processors, use `Synth.getEffect()` or `Synth.getModulator()` and call `getAttribute()` on the returned handle.
