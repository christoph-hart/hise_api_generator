Enables or disables the high-precision grid timer at a specific musical subdivision. The tempo factor is an index from 0 (whole note) through 18 (1/64T triplet) -- common values include 8 (1/8 note), 11 (1/16 note), and 5 (1/4 note). This is a global setting. You must enable the grid before `setOnGridChange()` callbacks will fire.
