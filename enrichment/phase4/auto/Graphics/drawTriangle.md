Draws a triangle outline within the specified area, rotated by the given angle in radians. The base orientation is an upward-pointing isosceles triangle (vertices at top-centre, bottom-left, bottom-right). The `angle` rotates from this orientation - for example, `Math.PI` produces a downward-pointing triangle. The `lineThickness` controls the stroke width. To draw a filled triangle, use `fillTriangle` instead.
