Adds a pie segment (wedge or ring section) to the path. The `innerCircleProportionalSize` parameter controls the shape: `0.0` creates a solid wedge from the centre, values between 0 and 1 cut out a proportional inner circle to create a ring segment, and `1.0` produces a zero-width ring (effectively an arc outline). This is useful for knob-style indicators where you want a filled ring rather than a stroked arc.
