Returns a new colour with the brightness (HSB value component) replaced by the given value (0.0-1.0, clamped). A brightness of 0.0 produces black regardless of hue and saturation; 1.0 produces the fully bright version. Hue, saturation, and alpha are preserved. For proportional dimming or brightening relative to the current brightness, use `Colours.withMultipliedBrightness()` instead.
