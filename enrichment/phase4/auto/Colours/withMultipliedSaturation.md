Scales the existing saturation (HSB saturation component) by the given factor rather than replacing it. A factor of 0.0 produces greyscale, 1.0 leaves the colour unchanged, and values above 1.0 increase saturation (clamped internally). Hue, brightness, and alpha are preserved. A common pattern is to tie the factor to a normalised parameter value, creating a visual link between a knob's position and the colour intensity of its arc.
