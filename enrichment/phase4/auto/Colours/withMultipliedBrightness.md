Scales the existing brightness (HSB value component) by the given factor rather than replacing it. A factor below 1.0 dims the colour, 1.0 leaves it unchanged, and values above 1.0 boost brightness (clamped internally). Hue, saturation, and alpha are preserved. This is useful for pre-computing LED on/off palette variants at init time or for brightening a colour on hover without desaturating it (which mixing with white would do).
