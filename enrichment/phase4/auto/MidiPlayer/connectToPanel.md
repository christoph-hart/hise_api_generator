Connects this player to a ScriptPanel for automatic UI updates. The panel receives `repaint()` calls when new MIDI content is loaded, when the track or sequence selection changes, when the sequence is cleared, and when events are flushed. To also repaint during playback (e.g. to draw a moving cursor), call `setRepaintOnPositionChange(true)`. Use `getNoteRectangleList()` and `getPlaybackPosition()` inside the paint routine to render the current state.
